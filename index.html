My Skills

User Research and Interviews

My background in journalism gave me a seamless transition into qualitative user research. I’m strong at putting together worthwhile questions and executing interviews.

Writing, Editing, and Communication

My biggest strength lies in writing and communication. This ranges from thoroughly explaining important aspects of UX, such as Problem Statements, to writing scripts for presentations or descriptions of projects.
